- Use Chapter 1 in the introductory or background section of the thesis.

- Use Chapter 3 in the methodology or implementation chapter.

## Scope of the Split

The split follows the current state of the repository and the conceptual direction described in `README.md`.

- Chapter 1 explains what functional coverage gaps are, why they are relevant, and why coverage reports alone are insufficient without environment context.
- Chapter 2 maps the target multi-agent architecture to the current repository and explains the workspace structure.
- Chapter 3 details the implemented skills, the custom retrieval agent, the vManager access layer, and the methodology used to ground analysis in the verification environment.
